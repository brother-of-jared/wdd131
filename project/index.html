Featured Destinations

Explore our top picks of must-visit spots across the islands.

Boracay Island

A world-renowned Philippine island paradise famous for its powdery white sand (White Beach), crystal-clear turquoise waters, stunning sunsets, and vibrant atmosphere, blending tranquil relaxation with exciting water sports like diving, kite surfing, and island hopping, plus a lively nightlife with beach bars and fire dancers, making it a top destination for all traveler types.

Coron, Palawan

A world-class tropical paradise famed for its dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear turquoise waters, hidden lagoons (like Twin Lagoons), pristine white-sand beaches, and vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life, making it a top destination for island hopping, diving (shipwrecks & reefs), snorkeling, kayaking, and exploring natural wonders like Kayangan Lake and Siete Pecados.

Intramuros, Manila

Intramuros is Manila's historic "Walled City," a prime tourist spot offering a journey back to the Spanish colonial era with its thick stone walls, cobblestone streets, grand churches (like San Agustin), historic forts (like Fort Santiago), and museums, providing a rich cultural and historical experience with a distinct old-world charm amidst modern Manila. Visitors explore its Spanish-era architecture, learn about Philippine history, and experience traditional transport like kalesas (horse-drawn carriages) or bamboo bikes, making it an essential destination for understanding the nation's past.

When to Visit

Dry season typically runs Nov–May. Best beach weather is Dec–Apr. Typhoons are more frequent Jun–Nov (peak Aug–Oct). Siargao surfing peaks Sep–Nov; Tubbataha diving is Mar–Jun only.

Getting Around

Domestic flights by Cebu Pacific, Philippine Airlines, and AirAsia connect hubs (Manila/Cebu) to islands. Ferries (2GO, Montenegro) link nearby islands. In-town: Grab, taxis, jeepneys, tricycles.

Practical Tips

ATMs common in cities; carry cash for islands. Local SIM/eSIM from Globe, Smart, DITO. Power 220V 60Hz (Types A, B, C). Dress modestly in rural areas. Reefs: use reef‑safe sunscreen.
